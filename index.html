State of the Map Asia 2026 Osaka

State of the Map Asia is a regional conference by the OpenStreetMap community.

Planned dates: September 6 (Sun) - 7 (Mon), 2026 Proposed host area: Nakazakichō, Kita-ku, Osaka

Overview

State of the Map Asia brings together OpenStreetMap volunteers, researchers, and practitioners to share mapping practices and open geospatial knowledge.

Vision

We aim to create a welcoming space for diverse participants and strengthen collaboration across Asia, including humanitarian mapping, civic tech, and open data communities.

Current Status

This website is a working draft. Information reflects the proposal stage, and confirmed details will be updated through future announcements.

Venue Plan (Proposal-Based)

Proposed Venue Nakazakichō Hall / Salon de AManTo 天人 (Osaka)
Expected Capacity Approx. 140 participants + 40 staff (about 180 total)
Facilities Wi-Fi, AV equipment, power outlets, barrier-free access
Participation Fee (Proposed) Free (advance registration required, limited capacity)

Community and Organizing Structure

The proposal envisions collaboration among the Japanese OpenStreetMap community, YouthMappers, HOT Japan, and CrisisMappers Japan related members.
